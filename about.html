Introduction
Tybert Investments (Pvt) Ltd t/a TyberCon Building and Hardware Solution established in 2019. We build from ground up. The company born out of a passion to be one of the best construction one-stop in Zimbabwe. It is a fast-growing integrated technical conglomerate which prioritizes quality services delivery, custom designs and customer satisfaction. It was founded by a businessman with a wealth of long construction, hardware, building supplies experience, strong financial, accounting and business management background.
